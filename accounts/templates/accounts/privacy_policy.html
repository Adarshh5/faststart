Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02-06-2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how you can access and control it, and more. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and usage-related data, including:

  • Your name (if provided), email, age, and city
  • Your English learning level or progress
  • How many times you use different features
  • Which parts of the website you use most frequently
  • Your saved content, uploaded content, and content you generate with AI
  • Data collected automatically like session data or preferences (via cookies)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Improve AI response quality
  • Add new features that better match user needs
  • Understand mistakes and reduce errors
  • Enhance your experience and convenience
  • Perform market research to grow and improve the platform

We do not sell your data to anyone.

3. Accessing and Managing Your Information

We are working on a page where users will be able to:

  • View the personal information they provided directly (like name, email, age, city)
  • Edit or delete saved words or definitions
  • Update personal info (if editable)

Please note: Not all information can be edited or deleted by the user. Some technical or system-related data is retained for analytics and platform integrity.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Some data may be kept indefinitely (for example, AI training-related patterns or platform usage statistics).

If a user deletes their account, some data will be deleted, but certain anonymized or system data may remain.

We use third-party databases to store user data, and while we aim to keep your data secure, we cannot guarantee 100% protection.

5. Cookies and Tracking Tools

We use cookies and session storage to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Temporarily store content (like your AI-generated content)
  • Remember your progress during your session

We do not use marketing cookies or third-party ad trackers.

JavaScript tools may use browser storage like sessionStorage or localStorage. You can manage or block cookies from your browser settings, but some features might stop working correctly.

6.Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. It is your responsibility to check this page for updates. If we make major changes, we may display a notice on the website, but we are not required to inform you directly.
